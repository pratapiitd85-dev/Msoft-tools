Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Msoft Tools

Are Msoft Tools completely free to use?

Yes, all tools on Msoft Tools are completely free to use. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone without any hidden costs or subscription fees.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you don't need to create an account to use most of our tools. You can access and use them directly without any registration. Some advanced features might require an account in the future.

Are my files safe when using Msoft Tools?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Files processed through our tools are not stored on our servers permanently. They are automatically deleted after processing to ensure your data remains secure.

What file formats are supported?

We support a wide range of file formats including JPG, PNG, PDF, DOC, DOCX, and many more. Each tool page specifies the supported formats for that particular tool.

How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team via email at msofttools@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.
